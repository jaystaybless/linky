I love to browse the web. I also love to use different devices. Whilst using different devices on a daily bases I face one major problem and I bet you do to. When moving from one device to another, I am unable to keep track of my bookmarks as they are all saved within the browser I am using.


To put an end to all my troubles I created this app, which will allow me me to create an archive of all the websites I wish to return to at a later date.


This app is simple and effective. It allows users to create Categories, Sub Categories and Links.


Keep your eyes on this baby as there will be a lot more functionality to come as it grows into a mature adult.


Please feel free to download. Don't hesitate to make any suggestions for future modifications / features.
